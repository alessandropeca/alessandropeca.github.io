I specialize in the study and evolution of active galactic nuclei (AGN) across various large-area surveys. My work involves several key projects focused on high-luminosity, heavily obscured AGN, both at high redshifts and in nearby galaxies. My expertise spans X-ray spectral/photometric analysis, AGN/galaxy SED fitting, multi-wavelength AGN counterpart matching, and the management of large catalogs and databases.
